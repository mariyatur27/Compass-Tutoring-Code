Compass Tutoring

Promoting academic success for students online

Compass Tutoring is an online tutoring platform that pairs students from all over the world with tutors who help them achieve their academic goals. Our tutors offer online classes in 6 different subjects to students in grades 1 to 10. Having been running since 2021, we are proud to have successfully served more than 30 different students across Canada and the USA.

know more

What do we do?

Format

Students who register for our tutoring sessions can choose to sign up for 1, 2 or 3 months of tutoring. All lessons are taught online, through Google Meet or Zoom, with the tutor using online tools such as Powerpoint, Google Documents, JamBoards, online whiteboards and more to teach the lessons.

Subjects

We offer online tutoring services in math, sciences, english, french, computer science (programming), as well as preparation for SAT. We cater our services to students in grades 1 to 10, as well as ESL (english as a second language) students. Students can register for a maximum of 4 subjects at the same time.

Lessons

All lessons are 1-hour long and are taught online. The duration of a lesson can be modified per request, but is not guaranteed. Students can register for a maximum of 5 lessons per week. The schedule of the lessons are decided between the tutor and the student once the tutoring session officially begins.

Our Process

Step 1

Getting Started

First, the student has to register for a tutoring session on our website. If you are not sure what you want to register for, you can sign up for a free 30-minute assessment with one of our team members and we will help you make the best decision.

Step 2

Finding a Tutor

Once you register, we will find you a tutor who best fits your needs and preferences. This process can take up to 1 week. When we pair you with a tutor, you will receive an email from our team and a separate introductory email from the tutor.

Step 3

Starting Your Lessons

Once you get in touch with your tutor, you will have your first free lesson. If that goes well and you choose to continue your lessons, you will have to complete your payment for the tutoring session, and you will be able to continue with your lessons.

What does it take to become
a tutor with us?

1.

Strong Academics

At Compass Tutoring, we take the quality of our services into a great consideration. All tutors who apply to work with us, must have at least 90% (equivalent to A) if they are a student, or a great knowledge and skills if they are a professional worker, in the subjects that they chose to tutor.

2.

Tutoring Experience

We prefer all of our tutors to have previous tutoring experience when applying to work with us. Any previous tutoring experience, whether it was volunteering or a part-time job is beneficial and would be considered a great asset. To verify your experience, you will be asked to teach a 10-minute lesson if you receive an interview.

3.

Great Organization Skills

This tutoring position requires great organization skills because you will have to manage your student’s lessons, homework, assignments and potentially a Google Classroom or a Google Folder. You must be able to maintain everything well organized, clean and straightforward to provide the best possible experience for your student.

4.

Responsibility and Reliability

This position requires someone who is very responsible and reliable. It will be up to you to come up with a lesson schedule with your student and make sure that both of you are following it. You will have to keep track of the amount of hours you work and of the lessons completed. You will be responsible for representing our company in the best light.
